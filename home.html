Volere

Emerging from a diverse tapestry of sounds, Volere (Tim Scholl), crafts a musical experience as eclectic and vibrant as the artists who inspire him. With a foundation in Contemporary Music from Southern Cross University and 20 years of playing original and cover shows up and down the east coast of Australia, Volere's journey through music is a testament to his dedication and passion.

Volere has graced stages from Lismore to the Sunshine Coast, weaving his narrative through a dynamic range of genres. His performances in the Shannon Beaumont Band, Pink Zinc, Rochelle Lees Band, and the Lydia Storey Trio, as well as his creative contributions to original groups like Headphone Symphony, Arora and Sujitoko, showcase his versatility and adaptability. In 2023, he embraced a solo career, eager to get back amongst the fray with a new perspective.

Influenced by legends from Chuck Berry to Bill Withers, and bands like Metallica to Snarky Puppy and everything in between. Volere's style is an amalgamation of rock, jazz, R+B, and pop, resonating with the finesse of classic and contemporary melodies. His idol, Bill Withers, anchors his artistic vision; Volere's stage name itself - Volere is Italian for 'will', a homage to the great composer himself.

Volere offers a range of performance setups, from intimate acoustic sessions to full-scale productions with his own meticulously crafted backing tracks. His goal is to connect deeply with every audience, from casual cafe gatherings to spirited evenings at the local RSL, making every performance a memorable encounter.

As Volere continues to evolve and bloom in his musical journey, he invites listeners to experience the heartfelt narratives and soulful grooves that define his sound.
